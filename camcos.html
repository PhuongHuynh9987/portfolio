I. Introduction

This project is a part of the CAMCOS (Center for Applied Mathematics, Computation and Statistics) course offered by the Department of Mathematics and Statistics at San Jose State University.
In this project, we are divided into two teams, a data and a theory team. While data team gathers data from BlockNative and performs data analysis and manipulation, theory team uses the information in addition to throughout researches about Ethereum blockchain system to analyze a possible solution, a forward inclusion list (FIL) design, imposed for censorship resistance. While the main project can be found here, I have put together a side analysis myself to find important insights that could help me with the research.
